Welcome to Barrio Bolívar

The neighborhood called Barrio Bolívar was one of the first populated areas of the city of David, Chiriquí, Republic of Panama. In its beginnings it had few houses that would form the town. The historic and cultural beauty can still be perceived. The oldest constructions that still exist to this day are dated since the XVIII Century; with a touch of history and mystery Barrio Bolivar represent the oldest town in Chiriqui. With its old roads made from stones, houses made from dirt and mud blocks and a tranquil ambiance, the town has its origins since the founding of David in 1602 by Juan Lopez Sequeira. Barrio Bolívar is 30 - 45 minutes by car to the wonderful mountains and beaches of the region.
