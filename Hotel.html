Welcome to the Nido Hotel

The Nido Hotel is a project of a micro hotel, that means only one room, a room with a marvelous view. Located on the back of Nido de las Artes Cultural Center and besides the Ab CAFFÈ, in the heart of the old town. The property is in the corner of a small rain forest and only some meters of the citytown, which means you can sleep and awake up seeing butterflies, listening the birds and the frogs and in minutes been in the city if that is your desire. The unique double room of Nido Hotel has an lovely interior design with beautiful handcrafted details comparing to the best boutique hotels. We are sure that you will have and amazing experience. The hotel is 30 - 45 minutes by car to the wonderful mountains and beaches of the region
