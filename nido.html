Nest of Arts

El Nido de las Artes (Nest of Arts) is an artistic, cultural and educational center in David, Chiriquí, Republic of Panama. The center has its beginnings in Brazil in the year 1994 and was develop in Chiriqui in 2013. The vision of the founders and the developers is for each Nido de las Artes to become the main source in the generator of arts, culture, education and communication in the local community. In addition, the project create physical spaces for all the artistic and cultural manifestations, incluing proper theaters buildings, alternative artistic spaces and art schools. The mission of Nido de las Artes is to awaken, stimulate, produce and project what is most positive and unique in the community in which it is inserted, as well as providing new artistic arsenals and techniques that qualify local production to a level of international competition, and promote the exchange between the arts, cultures, peoples and races.
